FAQs

Q. How many years has the Whitetail Almanac predicted the daily feeding and travel patterns of the white-tailed deer?
A. Launched in 1997 in a calendar format, the Whitetail Almanac has been helping hunters be at the right place and at the right time, for the past 24-years.
Q. Who developed the Whitetail Almanac?
A. The current President and CEO of Hunter’s Edge, LLC., Jamie Bulger.
Q. Where did the information come from, which predicts the detailed feeding and travel patterns of the white-tailed deer?
A. From radio telemetry studies, independent studies, and personal observations.
Q. How accurate is the Whitetail Almanac?
A. Our studies have shown that approximately 70% of deer movement takes place during the times predicted.
Q. What dictates changes in the 70% deer movement prediction?
A. Changes in the weather, hunting pressure, and predator movement, reduce the effectiveness of the Whitetail Almanac by approximately 30%
Q. How does the rut effect the Whitetail Almanac?
A. Peak movement times intensify during the pre-rut, rut, and post-rut periods.
Q. What is the Hunting Journal, and when was it developed?
A. The Hunting Journal gives a hunter the ability to record detailed events that take place during their hunt. Its original design was created in 2002 by Jamie Bulger in paperback form.
Q. Why is it important to record information about my hunts?
A. Climate, terrain, wind direction, food source, moon position, barometric pressure, and rutting behavior will differ from region to region. As you record your hunts, you will begin to see various patterns emerge, which will help you form strategies to help you “Outwit the deer you’re hunting.” NOTE: The most important entry you can record, is the time you first see a deer from your stand. It doesn’t matter what sex the deer is, but it’s critical to know when the first sighting takes place. We will compile this information from hunters across the county, to make sure the Whitetail Almanac Algorithm is adjusting the peak movement times to each hunters GPS location. Good Luck! Jamie Bulger President/CEO Hunter’s Edge, LLC
