Privacy Policy

Hunter's Edge, LLC., a Georgia corporation and owner of the Whitetail Almanac (collectively referred to as "the Almanac" welcomes you to the Electronic Version of the Whitetail Almanac Application (the "App"). The App is designed for YOU, and other hunters who want to know the Daily Feeding and Travel Patterns of White-tailed Deer. The App also provides YOU the opportunity to record information relating to your hunting of those Whitetail Deer. Only YOU can share activity information with those YOU trust. Your data is very important to us in relationship to the hunting of Whitetail Deer. Thus, by using the App, you give us the right to use your collective information, not for distribution to other hunters for their personal use, but to use as a tool to generate collective statistics, which will in turn allow us the opportunity to further study the movement and rut patterns of the White-tailed Deer.
